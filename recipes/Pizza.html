Pizza Recipe

Description

A classic Italian favorite, pizza consists of a thin or thick crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. Each slice is a perfect blend of flavors, making it a beloved choice worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded or sliced)
  • Pepperoni (or other meats)
  • Bell peppers (sliced)
  • Oregano (dried or fresh)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Roll out pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake until crust is golden.
