Döner Recipe

Description

A delicious Turkish dish featuring seasoned meat, typically lamb or chicken, sliced thin and served in a warm pita or flatbread. It's topped with fresh vegetables and savory sauces for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Lamb or chicken meat (thinly sliced)
  • Pita or flatbread
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (sliced)
  • Lettuce (e.g., iceberg or arugula)
  • Sauce (e.g., yogurt or garlic sauce)

Steps

  1. Marinate meat with spices and grill or cook until done.
  2. Slice the meat thinly.
  3. Warm pita bread.
  4. Assemble with meat, onions, tomatoes, lettuce, and sauce.
