Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagne is a hearty Italian pasta dish made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese, all baked to perfection. It's a comforting and filling meal that brings a taste of Italy to your table.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or uncooked)
  • Ground meat (beef or a mix)
  • Tomato sauce (canned tomatoes or jarred sauce)
  • Béchamel sauce (butter, flour, milk)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (or other cheese)
  • Onions (chopped)

Steps

  1. Cook ground meat with onions and tomato sauce.
  2. Layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, and béchamel in a baking dish.
  3. Top with cheese.
  4. Bake until bubbly and golden.
